Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for the rafting business is to enhance visibility, engage potential customers, and streamline various aspects of the business.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on): The target customers for the water rafting website are adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers aged 18-40. They are physically active individuals who enjoy outdoor activities and are seeking unique and adrenaline-pumping whitewater rafting experiences. Families with children looking for family-friendly adventures and corporate groups interested in team-building experiences are also part of the target audience. What do they want?: Exciting and memorable whitewater rafting experiences. Family-friendly adventures for those with children. Customizable team-building options for corporate groups. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Desktop and Laptop: Potential customers might access the website from their desktop or laptop devices when planning and researching rafting adventures. Mobile Device: Customers on the go, especially younger adults, may use their mobile devices to access the website for quick information, checking availability, and making reservations.
